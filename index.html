Michelle Obama: An exceptional woman

An attorney, a writer and a mother

Here's a timeline of Michelle Obama's life:

  • On November 4, 2008, she became the first African American first lady.
  • Born January 17, 1964, Chicago, Illinois, U.S. to Marian and Frasier Robinson
  • Studied sociology and African American studies at Princeton University (B.A., 1985) in New Jersey
  • Attended Harvard Law School (J.D., 1988).
  • Junior associate at Sidley & Austin (now Sidley Austin LLP)
  • Became an assistant to Chicago Mayor Richard M. Daley.
  • As first lady, Michelle was involved in various causes, notably supporting military families and ending childhood obesity.
